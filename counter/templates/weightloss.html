Food Items To Limit

Sugary foods and beverages
Processed and packaged foods
High-calorie snacks
High-carb condiments and sauces
Alcohol

Do's And Dont's

Do's:
Eat foods rich in, or fortified with calcium
Pair iron-rich foods with vitamin C
Be mindful of protein: About 20% of your total calories should come from protein

Don'ts:
Do not eat processed sources of protein
Do not consume sugary desserts and sodas Do not get stuck in a diet rut
